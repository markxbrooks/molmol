NAME

TintPrim - change secondary color of primitives
SYNOPSIS

TintPrim expr expr expr
DESCRIPTION

Set the secondary color of the selected primitives. The color is given by its Red, Green and Blue components, all in the range between 0.0 and 1.0.

The secondary color is used for drawing ribbons in two colors and for one side of sheets.

EXAMPLE

TintPrim 1.0 0.0 0.0
