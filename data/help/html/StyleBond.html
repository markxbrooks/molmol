NAME

StyleBond - set display style of bonds
SYNOPSIS

StyleBond ( invisible | line | half_line |
    cylinder | half_cylinder | neon | half_neon |
    cone | half_cone )
DESCRIPTION

Set the display style of all selected bonds. The style 'cylinder' draws the bond as an open cylinder, 'neon' draws it as cylinder with round ends, 'cone' as cone, taking the radii of the connected atoms.

The styles 'half_line', 'half_cylinder', 'half_neon' and 'half_cone' draw the bonds in two parts, taking the colors of the connected atoms.

EXAMPLE

StyleBond cylinder
