SplitRibbon

NAME

SplitRibbon - split ribbons
SYNOPSIS

SplitRibbon
DESCRIPTION

Split all selected ribbons into two pieces of equal length. The length of the pieces can later be adjusted with LengthPrim.

To get rid of a ribbon, use the RemovePrim command.

EXAMPLE

SplitRibbon
SEE ALSO

SelectPrim, LengthPrim, RemovePrim
